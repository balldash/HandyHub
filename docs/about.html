About HandyHub

HandyHub was inspired by the need to improve the way local tradesmen advertise their services in my community. Growing up, I saw many service providers relying on flyers and street posts, which were often ineffective. I wanted to create a platform that empowers tradesmen and makes it easier for clients to find skilled professionals. This project also gave me the opportunity to use my engineering skills to contribute to my community.

HandyHub is also my Portfolio Project for Holberton School, where I am learning full-stack development. The project has evolved from a simple idea to a full-featured application aimed at solving real-world problems. You can follow its progress and see the full codebase on GitHub.

Timeline: The project started in mid-2024 and is continuously evolving with new features and improvements.
