About HandyHub

HandyHub is a community-driven platform designed to connect clients with skilled tradesmen in their local area. Our goal is to provide an easy-to-use directory where users can quickly find trusted professionals for various services such as plumbing, carpentry, electrical work, and more.

Project Vision

Our vision is to empower local service providers by giving them a digital space to showcase their skills, while making it easier for people to find reliable tradesmen without relying on traditional advertising methods such as flyers or street posts.

Key Features

  • Searchable directory of tradesmen by location and specialization.
  • Profiles for tradesmen including name, email, location, and skills.
  • User-friendly mobile-first design for easy access on any device.

Team Members

HandyHub is developed by a dedicated solo engineer passionate about using technology to improve the community. The project is part of a larger school assignment, which emphasizes backend development and practical problem-solving.

Timeline

The project started in mid-2024 and is progressing steadily, with features like user directories, filtering, and design layouts currently under development.
